輔助變數（為 p、r、d 的函數）
Helper Variables (Functions of p, r, d)

設 \( P' = \lceil |p| \rceil \)，\(\; R' = \max(3, \lceil r \rceil)\)，\(\; D' = \max(2, \lceil |d| \rceil) \)。
Let \( P' = \lceil |p| \rceil \), \(\; R' = \max(3, \lceil r \rceil)\), \(\; D' = \max(2, \lceil |d| \rceil) \).

設 \( k = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{若 } P' < 3 \\ \lceil \log_2(P' \cdot \frac{4}{3}) \rceil - 2 & \text{否則} \end{cases} \)
Let \( k = \begin{cases} 0 & \text{if } P' < 3 \\ \lceil \log_2(P' \cdot \frac{4}{3}) \rceil - 2 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \)

設 \( c = D' - 1 \)
Let \( c = D' - 1 \)

設 \( N' = \max(4, \lceil n \rceil) \)
Let \( N' = \max(4, \lceil n \rceil) \)

設 \( \text{BinomInv}(n, p_{prob}, \alpha) \) 為符合二項式累積分佈函數 \( F_B(x; n, p_{prob}) \ge \alpha \) 的最小整數 \( x \)。
Let \( \text{BinomInv}(n, p_{prob}, \alpha) \) be the smallest integer \( x \) such that the Binomial CDF \( F_B(x; n, p_{prob}) \ge \alpha \).

支援人數範圍
Supported Player Range

\[ \max\left(4, \; P', \; \left\lfloor \frac{3}{4} \cdot 2^{R' - k - c - 1} \right\rfloor + 1 \right) \le n \le \max\left(4, \; P', \; \left\lfloor \frac{3}{4} \cdot 2^{R' - k} \right\rfloor \right) \]

最多負局數
Maximum Losses

\[ L_{max} = \begin{cases} \text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right) & \text{若 } N' \cdot \frac{4}{3} \cdot F_B\left(\text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right); R', 0.5\right) \le P' \\ \text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right) - 1 & \text{否則} \end{cases} \]

\[ L_{max} = \begin{cases} \text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right) & \text{if } N' \cdot \frac{4}{3} \cdot F_B\left(\text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right); R', 0.5\right) \le P' \\ \text{BinomInv}\left(R', 0.5, \frac{P'}{N' \cdot \frac{4}{3}}\right) - 1 & \text{otherwise} \end{cases} \]
